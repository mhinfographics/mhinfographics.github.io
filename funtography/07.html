Selenge Delta, Republic of Buryatia

The Selenge/Selenga River Delta, is one of the largest inland freshwater floodplains in the world. There are amazing satellite images of this place, not much SAR data tho or at least not that I know of. The resolution of the data on these areas is actually low from Open Source data, and that's why I picked up this place to try a Voxcel Theme on it.

I think games and art are an awesome source of inspitarion for these type of maps. Not sure if I'll be ever able to publish for real a thing like this, but who knows... maybe I should give a try.

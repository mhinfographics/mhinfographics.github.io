Bali, Indonesia

The paradisiac Bali. In terms of the island's economy, tourism is the most important. Bali is one of the richest regions in Indonesia. For the past 20 or 30 years, the island has been turning to tourism, according to media such as Time Magazine.

However, tourists have brought some development to the island, but as with other natural paradises, this comes at a price.

A few years ago I spent a few days between Denpasar and Nusa Dua, and I can attest to how wonderful this place is. Perhaps those of us who visit these natural paradises should be more aware of what our days of joy can represent for the locals.

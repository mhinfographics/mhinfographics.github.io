Singapore

A few years ago, while working for Reuters, I lived for a few years on the warm and futuristic island of Singapore. I have very fond memories of that time: long walks along Marina Bay, the Gardens by the Bay, that place on Dempsey Hill where I used to take my family all the time, relaxing in the pool in my place in Clementi, or visiting Westgate and Jem, even the airport...

To my friends there, I hope you all are doing great, living the good life. You are always in my thoughts.
